### Minor: 1

#### MINOR-1: `MAX_CONSECUTIVE_ERRORS = 5` may be too aggressive for unreliable RPC endpoints

**File:** `backend/services/monitor.py:39`
**Section:** Config constants

**Detail:** The monitor skips a wallet after 5 consecutive errors. RPC endpoints (Etherscan, Solscan, Blockchair) can experience transient outages lasting several minutes. With POLL_INTERVAL=60s, 5 consecutive errors = ~5 minutes of consecutive failure before the wallet is skipped. During this period, 5 failed fetches block the semaphore slot, reducing throughput for other wallets.

**Impact:** Low — the wallet will be retried on the next successful cycle (errors reset on success). The 5-error skip is a circuit-breaker that prevents one permanently-broken wallet from consuming all semaphore slots forever.

**Recommendation:** Consider raising to 10 (≈10 minutes of consecutive failures) to be more tolerant of transient RPC outages. This is a minor tuning change, not urgent.

**Decision:** DEFERRED — current value is acceptable. Worth reviewing when more wallets are tracked.
